Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025
TikTok Roulette is a personal, non-commercial tool. It does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. This policy explains exactly what data is accessed and why.

1. What We Access

When you connect your TikTok account, we access the following data through TikTok's official API:

  • Basic profile information — your display name and profile picture, used solely to show you who is logged in.
  • Your liked videos list — used to randomly select one video and display its link to you.

2. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not store any of your data on any server.
  • We do not share your data with any third party.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics.
  • We do not display ads.
  • We do not build profiles or retain any information after your session ends.

3. How Data Is Handled

All data retrieved from TikTok's API is processed exclusively in your browser (client-side). Nothing is transmitted to or stored on any external server operated by this application. When you close the tab or log out, all data is discarded.

4. Authentication

Login is handled via TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 protocol. We never see, handle, or store your TikTok password. Access tokens are stored temporarily in your browser's session storage and are automatically deleted when your session ends.

5. Third-Party Services

This application communicates only with TikTok's official API (open.tiktokapis.com). No other third-party services, analytics platforms, or tracking tools are used.

6. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact

This is a personal project. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the GitHub repository associated with this project.
